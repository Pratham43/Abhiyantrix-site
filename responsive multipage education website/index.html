What is call for Paper?

A "call for papers" is an invitation or announcement made by a conference organizer, inviting researchers, scholars, experts, and practitioners to submit original research, case studies, or other contributions to be presented at the conference. The call for papers typically outlines the conference theme or topics, submission guidelines, deadlines, and any specific requirements for formatting or length. It provides potential contributors with important information about the conference and what is expected of their submission.
